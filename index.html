About Letter to Sky

"Those we love never truly leave. They wait in the spaces between our words."

Why this place exists

Grief is one of the most universal human experiences — and one of the loneliest. Letter to Sky was born from a simple belief: that love doesn't end at death. The words we never said, the stories we still want to share — they deserve a place to go.

This is not about letting go. It is about holding on, beautifully, in a way that honors both the living and the remembered.

How it works

Write your letter as if they can hear every word — because in your heart, they can. Choose whether to keep it private, a sacred message between you and them, or share it publicly to offer comfort to others walking a similar path.

Every letter in our gallery was given freely, with love. Reading them reminds us we are not alone in our missing.

A safe and gentle space

All public letters are reviewed with care before being shared. Our small team reads every one with respect and gratitude. Letters that are private remain yours alone — we will never share them.
